This distribution reorganizes the upstream research code under the MediLife repository, renames product-facing scripts and configurations to MediLife-R1, separates generated upstream examples from reproducible source, and adds static validation. The underlying `verl` package boundary is retained for compatibility with EasyR1/veRL.

No claim is made that the upstream GPU training runs, model checkpoints, leaderboard results, or paper metrics were reproduced as part of MediLife.
